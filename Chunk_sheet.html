Personal Facts


Concept | A cinnamon roll
Chunk is just kind of a nice guy. He's a little native, a little delusional but overall has his heart in the right place - which is a rare occurrence in the World of Darkness and makes him - in his very own way - quite unique.

Name | Barney Chunk
His full name is Barnaby Mortimer Chunk, but since high school, his friends (and enemies) have been calling him simply Chunk. Maybe because he's always had a sweet tooth an it shows but 'Barnaby Mortimer' is also such an old man name, Chunk is just kind fo snazzier.
Since his the beginning of his security guard career, he's also really taken to Officer Chunk
Age | *1958
As of 2005, Chunk is 47 years old and looks like it.

Origin | Whittier, Los Angeles County, CA, United States of America
Chunk is a born Angeleno and has never really left Los Angeles metropolitan area either.

Nature |
Optimist The Optimist knows things could be a lot worse, but with a little effort they're going to get better. He sees the positive side of having survived death, and presses the realization on everyone. The Optimist vigilantly aims to keep spirits high, helping to show others that even in the Underworld, it's not just doom and gloom. This, in his opinion, is the best way to fight Oblivion and its sidekick, despair.
— The Optimist regains Willpower every time he discovers and champions the bright side to an otherwise grim event.
(Wraith20 p. 129)


Demeanor |
Bureaucrat The Bureaucrat works the system from within. He recognizes the need for regulations, forms, and ordered queuing. Patience and organization are typical strengths of the Bureaucrat, who follows every procedure. The Bureaucrat understands rules and red tape can stifle initiative, but only by working steadily via the correct channels can security be maintained.
— The Bureaucrat regains Willpower every time he uses the established rules to deal with a situation.
(Wraith20 p. 126)
