Personal Facts


Concept | Ghost Council with a Winter's Teeth Heart
River has the grit, the willpower and the pain-tolerance of a Winter's Teeth, having grown up in the cold northern regions of Alaska. However she is a member of the Ghost Council,and knows that sometimes you have to get dirty to get the job done. She's a perfect mixture of brains and brawn and I created her purposefully so that she is the polar opposite of my last Ghost Council character; who was shy and needed to find her voice.
River does not need to find her voice. She she is self-assured, self-confident and knows what she wants.

Name | Chelsea Anderson, Hunts-by-the-River
Her birthname is Chelsea Anderson and is called Sia by her mother and childhood friends to this day. to everyone else she is known by her Garou Name Hunts-by-the-River, or River for short, which she took after leaving her maternal ancestral home in Alaska to embark on the journey of becoming a member of the Garou Nation and the Ghost Council in paricular.

Tribe | Ghost Council
Born on Winter's Teeth territory, it should have been clear from the start whom she was going to join. River is indigenous and part of the Alaskan First Nations people. However, she was born with Ghost Council Pure Breed. That caused a bit of a political kerfuffle, especially if you consider that her mother is not kin. But it was decided for her that it might be best to go with the ancestors who shine so strongly through her blood and she joined the Ghost Council after her Rite of Passage.

Breed | Homid
River was born to a non-kin human woman. She has no idea who her father is, but he is either Garou or kin.

Auspice | Philodox
Born under a half-moon and with a Tribal dispute as soon her First Change came about, he seemed to have been born to be a mediator. River is very occupied with the truth and with what's right but is very aware of the many nuances and complication on the way.

Age | 28, born 1973
Child of the 70s. Considering the high mortality rate of Garou, River is getting into a more respectable age.

Origin | Eagle, Alaska, United States
Born to the Hän Hwëch'in First Nation, right next to the Canadian border, she calls the cold wilderness of the Arctic her home. She doesn't actually know, who her father is; her mother doesn't really like talking about it, but her grandma said that he's a failed Indian rapper and don't ever trust a guy from the rez with an animal in his name.
