Personal Facts


Concept | Femme Fatale with an abusive Daddy
I have essentially played this character before and I was never able to end her story properly, with has always bothered me greatly because I loved that character. As such, I am reheating the old concept - a beautiful, smiley princess with a vicious sociopathic hidden character. Abused all of her life she craves power and to subdue those who wronged her (or rather those who remind her of those who wronged her) while being controlled by her powerful, yet abusive Sire.

Name | Leila Monroe
Leila Ibraheem Khoury changed her name to Lily Monroe as soon as she entered Hollywood. Too yuckily foreign was her birth name, that it had to be changed so that she would have any chance of fame at all. She never fully look to "Lily", though, always thought of herself as Leila. That is why nowadays she goes by her real first name and her stage last name.

Clan | Lasombra
Leila will let you know that she was embraced in the old-school Lasombra tradition: a Sire puts their hand-selected prospective progeny through unimaginable troubles to see whether they rise to the occasion despite hardship. She was her Sire's long-time tortured Ghoul, that much is true; she however was embraced in true Shovelhead fashion.

Generation | 10
Official from LA by Night.
Embraced by California's Archibishop, an ellusive man known as The Caballero, Leila was fortunate enough to have been embraced with a relatively low gen, compared to many of the modern day Kindred.
