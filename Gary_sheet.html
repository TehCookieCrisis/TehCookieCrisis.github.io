Personal Facts


Concept | The Cleopatra turned Spymaster
Gary once was a old Hollywood filmstar, famously fleet footed and dashingly handsome one of the shiniest stars in the L.A. night sky. However, all good things must come to an end. Gary was already ageing and losing some of the stamina and charm that had made him famous back in the day. His Embrace however... well let's say he gained stamina at a steep price.
It's a cruel joke and Gary never quite got over, but he recognizses that it was a blessing in some ways. Mr Golden had been swimming with sharks for nearly his entire life and he knows how the game is played. You don't become a Hollywood legend without knowing how to direct the pieces on the board and learn the value of information. In the grand scheme of things, Gary is not that old for a vampire. But his incredible charisma, his wittiness and a certain ruthlessness have catapulted him to the top of the hierarchy within the L.A. Nosferatu.

Age | *1905 - †1962
Gary was an ageing Hollywood legend when we was embraced. In theory he is permanently stuck at 57 years old, although that's as good a guess as any, when looking at him. Gary actually thinks being a Nosferatu made him look a little younger. Who knew looking like a sleep paralysis demon could gave you a facelift?
