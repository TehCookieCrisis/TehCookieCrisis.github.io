Personal Facts


Concept | The Drug Mule
Brie is a party girl, a slut, an idiot, a drug addict, a Valley girl and one of the few souls in the West who can brew an incredibly potent potion that can confer the power of Disciplines without ever learning them. Sadhana is an old Indian form of blood magic and is jealously guarded by the bloodlines in Southeast Asia. Older and in some ways more versatile than a Tremere's Thaumaturgy, there is a catch - it is only effective if the caster does not take monetary compensation for his craft. And this Brianna was embraced.
Brianna was created to brew the potions so that her Sire may profit off of her labor. She doesn't really see it that way, mostly because she is somewhat lacking in the brains department, but she is also just a simple woman with simple desires; and her Sire is just very good at giving her what she wants: The freedom to live life as a neverending party and all the drugs she could possibly desire.

Name | Brianna "Brie" Danielle Wydman
They thought Brian Daniel was going to be boy - however a crying baby girl was put into the arms of the Wydmans shortly after birth and they didn't really feel like changing the name they had already picked out.

Clan | Daitya Malkavian
Brianna? Embraced into a rare Setite Bloodline? That has a strong belief in the Indian caste system and rarely if ever embraces outside of it?
Rumors only! Never would her Sire have made a vampire that would land him a Final Death sentence as soon as they are found out! Never! And now stop asking questions, this is clearly a Malkavian!

Generation | 14
The moment anyone finds out about her Generation, people are genuinely surprised that her blood is strong enough to manifest her Clan at all. By all metrics, this should be a Thin-Blood.

Sect | Followers of Set Independent
Brianna is nominally part of the Anarchs because they currently rule Los Angeles, but she would swear fealty to the next big kahuna if that's what it took to be left to her own devices. She is not really interested in politics and that probably saved her ass more times than she is aware of.
She is technically a member of the crazy sex-cult of the Setites, but that technicality is only due to her life-style and bare minimum adherence to the cult by her Sire. He up and left India and has no intention of ever returning. As such they both masquerade as Malkavians - which thankfully is a famously fickle Clan with no true allegiance save to whatever demons hound their heads at present.

Age | *1948 - †1969
Despite her youthful appearance of 21 years, she is actually a Baby Boomer. She was Embraced during the Woodstock festival in 1969, which makes her 57 years of age in total, and 36, if you count only her unlife, that makes her a somwhat older Neonate.

Origin | Glendale, California, United States of America
Brianna actually is an L.A. native and never really left. Sure, she toured the continental United States and bits of Canada here and there, but eventually she always came back to the city of angels.

Nature |
Bon Vivant The Bon Vivant knows that life — and unlife — is shallow and meaningless. As such, the Bon Vivant decides to enjoy her time on Earth. The Bon Vivant is not necessarily irresponsible. Rather, she is simply predisposed to having a good time along the way. Most Bon Vivants have low Self-Control ratings, as they are so given to excess. Hedonists, sybarites, and dilettantes are all examples of the Bon Vivant Archetype. A Kindred Bon Vivant may sire a brood of fawning childer, or he may spend his time gorging on the blood of drug abusers for the contact high.
— Regain a point of Willpower whenever you truly enjoy yourself and can fully express your exultation. At the Storyteller's option, a particularly fabulous revel may yield multiple Willpower points.
(V20 Core p. 88)

Brianna is not here for a long time, but a good time. Never having quite internalized eternity, Brianna is a very human-like Kindred, in that she still kind of operates on the principle of I am young now, so yolo. That does have its perks, for she is much closer to Humanity that most vampires and gives her a certain charme, even if that doesn't make er seem particularly bright.
Brianna is a hedonist, much like most of the members of her Clan, and she doesn't really like doing things that are not fun to her.

Demeanor |
Addict The Addict simply can't get enough of a good thing. Unlike the Bon Vivant, who finds pleasure where she can, the Addict turns a specific into an obsession, giving it up only for a stronger urge. Obviously, this may be a narcotic, but it can also be a certain person, place or activity that has significance. Gamblers, workaholics and stalkers are all good examples of the Addict Archetype.
— Regain Willpower when you are able to gorge yourself on your chosen passion.
(Hunter - The Reckoning p. 98)

Brianna is a product of her time - and in her time LSD trips had just gotten really popular. She was an addict in life and nothing changed in unlife. She was probably lucky to have been embraced while still being young and beautiful otherwise she might have died a sad early death with an emaciated body.

You gotta know where to get the good stuff and not the shit laced with Fentanyl and laundry detergent. A rich friend
Brianna is famously alwyas broke, but she likes to party hard. The solution is a rich friend, who keeps her around like a weird pet. For as long as Brianna is entertaining, she will be invited to the parties of the rich and famous (and those who pretend to be).


A professional squatter
Brianna knows this one guy who like always finds a way to have a roof over his head. From crackhuts to the summer home of a rich Saudi prince, he knows how to get into places. He's not a burglar though, he doesn't steal anything, he just eats everything in the fridge, sleeps in every bed and pisses into the pool. The moment the owners come back, he bolts. If Brianna is a in a pinch and needs somewhere to stay for a few days, he's usually helped her out so far.
